Audience
Who are your ideal users?
- Our ideal users are ucsb students who are looking to get more insight on their future courses.
  
How many different kinds of users are there, and what are their roles?
- All the users would be ucsb students, but there could be different majors, years, and even prospective students potentially. Their roles are to gain more information and streamline the extensive research process behind course plannning. 

What will each different kind of user be able to do in your app?
- Everyone has the same type of scope in this project, there wouldnt be any "specific" experience other than the responses. 

What are their goals in using their app?
- This will enhance the experience by giving students comprehensive reviews of data compiled about courses and also help UCSB Gold be more streamlined in general.

How does your app allow them to accomplish their goal?
- This app allows students to accomplish their goal by doing all of the data collection they need for them; and providing a smart, detail filled answer regarding their courses. 

What technologies did you decide to use for your implementation? 
- FastAPI, OpenAI, Pinecone, Python, React

Why?
- We used FastAPI in order to create the API for all of our calls. OpenAI will be used for the backbone of our course assistant. Pinecone to help achieve RAG implementation. Python and React for the frontend and backend, and for any scripts.
